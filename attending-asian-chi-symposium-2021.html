Deadlines

Gary Marsden Travel Awards March 9, 2021
Early Registration March 16, 2021
Regular Registration March 17 - April 13, 2021
Onsite April 14 - May 13, 2021

This year, the participants of virtual 5th Asian CHI Symposium 2021 in conjunction to CHI 2021 benefits from:

  • a unique opportunity of enjoying reduced registration fees for everyone,
  • further reduced rates for individuals residing in most economically developing Asian countries,
  • and also generous Gary Marsden Travel Awards for students.

The registration rate for the 2-day symposium is $30, and it requires the attendees to register for at least one day of the conference. For enjoying a significantly reduced early registration rate, please register before or on March 16, 2021.
